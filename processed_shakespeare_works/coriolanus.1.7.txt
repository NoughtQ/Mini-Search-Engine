scene vii the gate of corioli titu lartiu have set a guard upon corioli go with drum and trumpet toward cominiu and caiu marciu enter with lieuten other soldier and a scout lartiu so let the port be guard keep your duti as i have set them down if i do send dispatch those centuri to our aid the rest will serv for a short hold if we lose the field we can not keep the town lieuten fear not our care sir lartiu henc and shut your gate upon our guider come to the roman camp conduct us exeunt
