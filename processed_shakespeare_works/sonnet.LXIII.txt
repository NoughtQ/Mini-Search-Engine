sonnet lxiii against my love shall be as i am now with time injuri hand crushd and oerworn when hour have draind hi blood and filld hi brow with line and wrinkl when hi youth morn hath travelld on to age steepi night and all those beauti whereof now he king are vanish or vanishd out of sight steal away the treasur of hi spring for such a time do i now fortifi against confound age cruel knife that he shall never cut from memori my sweet love beauti though my lover life hi beauti shall in these black line be seen and they shall live and he in them still green
